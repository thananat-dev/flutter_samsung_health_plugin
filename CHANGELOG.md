## 0.0.1

* Initial release of the Samsung Health plugin.
* Added support for connecting to Samsung Health Data SDK.
* Implemented permission requests for health data types.
* Added support for reading real-time metrics:
    - Steps, Heart Rate, Sleep.
    - Blood Oxygen (SpO2), Blood Glucose, Blood Pressure.
    - Body Composition, Water Intake, Floors Climbed.
    - Skin and Body Temperature, Nutrition, and Exercise.
* Implemented historical data reading for all supported metrics.
* Added Auto Sync foreground service for backend data synchronization.
* Added functionality to list connected health devices.
